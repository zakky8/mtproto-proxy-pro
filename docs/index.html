FAQ

Are these Telegram proxies free?

Yes. Every proxy here is a free, public MTProto proxy collected from open sources, then verified by our bot. There is no cost, signup, or app to install.

What is an MTProto proxy?

MTProto is Telegram's own protocol. An MTProto proxy relays your Telegram traffic, which helps you reach Telegram where it is blocked or throttled. It only affects Telegram, not your whole device.

How do I connect on iPhone or Android?

Tap Connect on any proxy. Your phone opens Telegram and asks to enable the proxy — tap “Connect/Enable proxy”. That's it.

How often is the list updated?

Every 6 hours. A GitHub Action re-collects, re-verifies, re-ranks and republishes the whole list, so dead proxies drop off automatically.

Is using a Telegram proxy safe?

A proxy operator can see that you connect to Telegram, but your messages stay end-to-end where Telegram already encrypts them. Prefer handshake ok proxies and avoid logging into sensitive accounts over an unknown proxy.
